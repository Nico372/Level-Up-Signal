Question of the month

HCPs are pulling back from paid social — so where should B2B healthcare marketers focus next?

Pharma / medtech marketers say their #1 challenge isn’t reaching HCPs — it’s knowing when they’re ready to engage. As attention shifts from paid social toward owned content and research, is the answer more data and AI-driven timing, or a fundamentally different content strategy?

Our take: The data points to owned content over rented attention. Brand websites, original research and thought leadership are picking up the slack — and they’re also what AI search tools tend to cite as trusted sources. The highest-leverage move may be a steady stream of credible, data-backed content that gets pulled into HCPs’ research, rather than pushed at them.
